Longterm Acute Care Component

NON-100% PLANS

GF PLAN:

- subject to applicable year deductible and coinsurance for the first days (IIN and OON). After 365 days, subject to deductible then BCBSAZ/Plan pays 50% member pays 50% for the remainder of the life of the plan, after initial 365 days, 50% coinsurance does not count toward OOP coinsurance maximum (S).
- subject to year deductible and coinsurance (N).

NON-GF PLAN:

- Subject to applicable year deductible and coinsurance for the first 100 days (INN and OON). Days 101-365, subject to deductible then BCBSAZ/Plan Pays 50%, member pay 50% (S).
- Subject to applicable year deductible and coinsurance for the first 365 days (IIN and OON). After 365 days, subject to deductible then BCBSAZ/Plan pays 50%, member pays 50% for the remainder of the life of the plan (N).
- Subject to year deductible and coinsurance (N).
